Welcome to the Climate Change Visualizer!

Would you like to learn about how your city or town has changed as a result of climate change over the past 100 years? Just input your address or the name of your city and country below and you can take a look at what the climate record shows.

You can put in a town/city and country, or a street address (No apartment numbers or postal codes though!).
